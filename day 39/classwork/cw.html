Dodge Hellcat

The Dodge Hellcat is a high-performance version of Dodge's muscle cars,
like the Challenger, Charger, and even the Durango SUV. It’s powered by
a supercharged 6.2-liter HEMI V8 engine, producing between 707 and over 800 horsepower,
making it one of the most powerful street-legal American cars. Known for insane
acceleration, aggressive styling, and loud engine roars, Hellcats are popular among car
enthusiasts who crave speed and raw power. Despite their size, they can go from 0 to 60
mph in just around 3.5 seconds and are
built for both straight-line performance and everyday use.
