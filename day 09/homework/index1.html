My favourite animal

My favourite animal is a cat. I think they are very cute and elegant.

There are various types of cats:

Wild cats

There are about 40 species of wild cats in the world.

Pantherinae (big cats)

Lions, Tigers, Leopards, Jaguars and Snow leopards

Felinae (small to medium-sized cats)

Cheetahs, Cougars (mountain lions), Lynxes, Ocelots and Servals

Domestic Cats

Maine Coon, Siamese, Persian, Bengal, Sphynx (hairless),Ragdoll and British Shorthair

Mythical and Folklore Cats

The Sphinx (Egyptian mythology)
The Maneki-neko (Japanese "beckoning cat")
The Cheshire Cat (from Alice in Wonderland)

cats in Egypt

History tells us that cats in Egypt were revered as sacred animals,
associated with the goddess Bastet, who symbolized protection and fertility.
Egyptians valued cats for their ability to control pests like rats and snakes,
and they were often kept as cherished companions.
Killing a cat was a serious crime, and families mourned their cats by shaving their eyebrows.
Cats were even mummified to accompany their owners into the afterlife,
showcasing their deep cultural and spiritual significance.

cats in Japan

Cats in Japan are symbols of luck, protection, and prosperity,
celebrated through icons like the Maneki-neko (beckoning cat). Valued for controlling pests,
they became beloved companions and inspired legends of supernatural cat spirits like the bakeneko.
Cats hold a special place in Japanese culture, art,
and folklore, embodying grace, mystery, and good fortune.
